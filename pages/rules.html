What do you need ?

  • ¤ 1 Timer
  • ¤ 3 Priority Fields *
  • ¤ 2 Life Point Counters **
  • ¤ 20 Green Cards*
  • ¤ 20 Natural Disasters*

* Those are provided in our printable kit.

** You may use a d20 or a paper and a pen instead.

Λ

How do you set the game ?

Three difficulty levels are available. Each difficulty level is associated with a specific time to finish the game. 

  • ¤ If you are playing as a team:

    1. - Beginner: 15 min, Nuck has 10 life points, you have 10. 
    2. - Amateur: 15 min, Nuck has 15 life points, you have 15. 
    3. - Expert: 10 minutes, Nuck has 15 life points, you have 5. 
  • ¤ If you are playing alone:
    1. - Beginner : 12 min, Nuck has 8 life points, you have 8.
    2. - Amateur : 12 min, Nuck has 12 life points, you have 12.
    3. - Expert : 8 minutes, Nuck has 15 life points, you have 5.
  • ¤ On the table you need:
    1. - In the middle of the table, put the 3 Priority Fields (1 = lower, 2 = medium, 3 = important), in the middle of the table.
    2. - Behind the priority fields, put Nuck Learrrh’s Space.
    3. - On Nuck Learrrh’s Space, set a pile of 15 Natural Disasters (question on top) and the red Life Points Counter on their dedicated spaces.
    4. - On the table in front of you, put your own Life Points Counter (the green one).
    5. - Next to it, add a pile of 20 Green Cards* – 15 if you are playing alone. The image must be on the top.

Feel free to make the game easier or more difficult by changing the limited time and / or life points totals (for Nuck and/or for you) !

Λ

Playing the game as a team :

  1. ¤ The youngest player starts. This player takes the card on the top of the Green Cards pile. The team (including the youngest player) tries to guess if it is lower / medium / higher priority.
    They put the card in the corresponding area.
  2. ¤ The youngest player turns the card around. Check your answer:
    • - If you are correct, Nuck the villain loses life equal to the priority level of your card.
    • - If you are wrong, Nuck attacks you* ! But you can resist if you find the correct answer to his Natural Disaster...
  3. ¤ If you find a Green Card’s question too hard, you may skip this card:
    • - When you skip a card, put it on the bottom of your pile.
    • - Be careful... If you don’t act for a long time and skip 3 cards in a row, Nuck will gain ground and attack you* with a Natural Disaster !
  4. And so on !
Λ

Playing the game alone :

  1. ¤ Take the card on top of your Green Cards pile.
  2. ¤ Read the principle on the card. Try to to guess if it is lower / medium / higher priority.
  3. ¤ Put the card in the corresponding area.
  4. ¤ Turn the card around. Check your answer:
    • - If you are correct, Nuck the villain loses life equal to the priority level of your card.
    • - If you are wrong, Nuck attacks you* ! But you can resist if you find the correct answer to his Natural Disaster...
    5. ¤ If you find a Green Card’s question too hard, you may skip this card:
    • - When you skip a card, put it on the bottom of your pile.
    • - Be careful... If you don’t act for a long time and skip 3 cards in a row, Nuck will gain ground and attack you* with a Natural Disaster !
  5. ¤ An so on.

* Check the “What happens when Nuck Learrrh attacks you ?” section. 

Λ

How does the game end ?

  1. ¤ If the allowed time is over and Nuck isn’t dead, Nuck Learrrh destroys the Earth and you (and everyone else) die !
  2. ¤ If Nuck deals you too much damage and your life points total is 0 or under, Nuck destroys the Earth and you (and everyone else) die !
  3. ¤ If Nuck’s life points drop to 0 or under 0, Nuck is dead and you win the game !
Λ

What happens if Nuck Learrrh attacks you with a natural disaster?

Nuck Learrrh attacks you with a natural disaster: 

  1. ¤ The oldest player takes a Natural Disaster card on top of the pile – hide the card’s back or everybody will see the answer.
  2. ¤ This player reads the question on the card to the team.
  3. ¤ The team (or alone player) tries to answer the question.
  4. ¤ The oldest player turns the card over. Check your answer:
    • - If you are correct, you resist to the Natural Disaster... for now...
    • - If you made a mistake, you take damage equal to the damage score on the card !
