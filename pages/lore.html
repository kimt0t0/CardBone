Card'Bon's Story...

Nuck Learrrh, a dangerous criminal, is threatening the planet with natural disasters!

The Gravediggers (you) must stop him and save the Earth. For this, you must be able to create sustainable projects. 


The more you show Nuck your capacity to tackle global warming by realizing the danger and making good choices, the more he loses strength and retreats! 


But be careful – if you make a mistake or if you are too slow, Nuck may send you storms, tsunamis and other catastrophees to kill you ! 


The future of the planet is entirely in your hands. You must save the Earth !
